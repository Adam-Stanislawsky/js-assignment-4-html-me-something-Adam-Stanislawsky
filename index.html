Blenko Collector Beginner Guide

Blenko Glass Company has been a family owned and operated company since 1893. They have been located in Milton, WV since 1921. Exquisite color, skilled craftsmen, and imaginative designs have made Blenko famous in the time-honored craft of hand-blown glass.

Why I collect:

  • Timeless design
  • Valuable
  • Easy/cheap to collect (if you know what to look for)

I believe it would be nice to have a simple guide for identifying Blenko pieces, as well as determining the color, model number, and year they may have been produced

My current rough method to identify a Blenko piece:

  • Check for pontil mark (not ALWAYS present)
  • Determine if it is a Blenko color
  • Browse through blenko catalogs to find possible match
  • Search ebay sold listings by model number and color to determine value

Blenko designers (not all, but created most sought after designs):

  • Winslow Anderson (1947-1953)
  • Wayne Husted (my favorite)(1952-1963)
  • Joel Myers(1963-1970)
  • Hank Murta Adams (my second favorite) (1988-1994)
